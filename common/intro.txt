Chapter 2
Returning to Earth with plans of enemy systems allowed to win long-lasting war.
For extraordinary achievement Robbo was granted the highest award - "Bolt of Valor".
Meanwhile scientists discovered anomalous meta-galaxy, where laws of physics seem to not apply.
Brave Robbo decided to join the research expedition and use extraterrestrial experience to scout this mysterious environment.
The journey took 30 light-years and in a moment Robbo is going to land on the first of the strange worlds...
